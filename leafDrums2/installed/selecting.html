Selecting drum or loop sounds

To select a drum or loop sound, first click on the line whose sound you want to change. If you want to add a new line, click on the last line, marked "(none)".

Then, on the centre left of the screen, click on the word "drum" if you want a drum sound, or "loop" if you want a loop.

Finally, choose the drum or loop you want using the list of drums/loops, and the scrollbar.

That sound will now be used for the selected line.
