Ordering information

leafDrums costs $20 US. Once you register (pay for the program), you will be given a code which removes the nag screens etc.

Registering for the program helps encourage me to write more music software, as well as being the Right Thing to do rather than using pirated or unregistered software. :)

Ordering online

If you have a credit card you can order securely online. This is the quickest and easiest way to order the program.

Ordering by mail

If you don't have a credit card or you don't have Internet access, you can also order by mail. Please print and follow the instructions on this form. You will need to send 20 US dollars in cash - we cannot accept cheques (the bank charges about $20 for us to pay in a US cheque, so it's not an option). If you don't live in the US, you should be able to easily purchase 20 US dollars from a bank, travel agent, or bureau de change.

If it's more convenient for you, you may alternatively pay in UK pounds sterling (the program costs 15 UK pounds). Again, this must be cash.

International mail can sometimes take some time - please allow at least 2 weeks for a response (if you give an e-mail address - more otherwise).

Ordering from within the UK

If you live in the UK, you may also pay by cheque drawn on a UK bank, for 15 pounds made payable to Samuel Marshall.

The dollar amount is (at time of writing) likely to be a little cheaper, so you are better off registering online if you have a credit card and internet access. (I encourage this because it's easier for me.)
